Basic Settings Guide

You can change the app theme in Settings > App Settings > App Theme. Options include System Default, Light, and Dark.

Go to Settings > App Settings > Daily Reminder to enable or disable daily hadith notifications.

In Settings > Reader Settings, you can customize your reading experience:

  • Hadith Layout: Switch between Horizontal and Vertical layouts.
  • Text Size & Style: Adjust the font size and style for Arabic and translation.
  • Hadith Text Option: Choose to show both Arabic and translation, or just one of them.
  • Show Sanad: Toggle to display the chain of narrators for each Hadith.

Go to Settings > Other > Resource Download Source to select your preferred server for downloading app resources (e.g., gh-proxy.alfaazplus.com).

About the Sunnah

The Sunnah refers to the traditions and practices of the Islamic prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) that constitute a model for Muslims to follow.

The Sunnah is what the Prophet (peace be upon him) said, did, or tacitly approved of. It is the second source of Islamic law after the Quran.

About SunnahApp

The SunnahApp is an ad-free and privacy-focused mobile application that facilitates exploring the Sunnah of the Prophet (peace be upon him).

It provides access to major hadith collections with translations, narrator chains, and scholar information, all in a clean, modern interface.
